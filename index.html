I’m Raph. These are some African masks from various ethnic groups that I’ve grown to be quite fond of, for one reason or another.

I wanted to show them to you mostly because they’ve been on my mind. Growing up, our house was full of them, and if I’m being honest, I thought they were terrifying. I really did.

Lately, though, I’ve felt this need to get a little closer to my roots. This whole project is just a small tribute to that feeling.

Anyway, that’s really all there is to it. I hope you get something out of them.

Best regards
